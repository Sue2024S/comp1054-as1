About

Released:2024

Artist: Renata Flores

Genre: Andean Pop

Why is this song meaningful to me?

This song is special for me because it expresses strength and resilience in the face of adversity. The lyrics evokes a profound connection with nature, reminding me of my roots and the enduring spirit of the earth. The use of Quechua, an indigenous language of Peru, and the fusion of traditional Andean music with modern pop beats creates a powerful and uplifting sound that I enjoy.

Artist

Renata Flores is Peruvian singer and rapper known for her innovative fusion of Andean music with contemporary genres like hip-hop, pop, and trap.

Renata is passionate about revitalizing the Quechua language and uses her music to address social issues faced by indigenous communities in Peru. Her lyrics often explore themes of cultural identity, empowerment, and environmental awareness.
